My Music

With 6 years of experience in music production, I create music that adapts to different styles and needs. About Me

I'm evyh! I make music and build websites. I've been producing music for 6 years, focusing on soundtracks and tracks that connect emotionally with each project. I like working across different genres and styles, always aiming to create something honest and impactful.

More recently, I’ve been getting into web development. I use HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create clean, functional websites that also have a strong visual identity. Whether it's for portfolios, art projects, or small businesses, I care about making things that feel right and work well.

If you’re looking for someone flexible with pricing, committed, creative, and straightforward who delivers quality that’s hard to find at an affordable rate, let’s talk.
